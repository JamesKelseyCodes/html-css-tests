Scandinavian Pizza

I bet you haven't tried the wonder that is the Scandinavian Pizza!

Sure, pizza may originate in Italy, but it was perfected in Sweden, Norway and Denmark!

You're going to need some ingredients to get started...

Ingredients

  • pizza dough mix
  • non-raising flour
  • banana's
  • hollandaise sauce
  • swedish style yurgen (1 Litre)

Now that's out of the way, we can begin...

Steps

  1. knead the dough (vigrously)
  2. carefully spread the hollandaise over the dough base
  3. generously top with hollandaise
  4. sprinkle the yurgen on top (careful not to use too much!)
  5. systematically place the sliced bananas on top
  6. place in pizza oven
  7. enjoy!
